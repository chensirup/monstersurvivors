About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you face endless waves of monsters. As the last hero standing, your mission is to survive as long as possible while defeating hordes of increasingly powerful enemies.

The game features a unique progression system where you collect experience orbs from defeated enemies to level up and choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones. Each run is different, with random power-ups and enemy spawns creating a fresh experience every time you play.

How To Play

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Automatically attack nearby enemies
  • Collect experience orbs to level up
  • Choose new abilities or upgrade existing ones when leveling up
  • Survive as long as possible!

Game Strategies

Success in Monster Survivors requires a balance between offensive and defensive abilities. Focus on movement speed early to avoid getting surrounded, then build up your damage output to handle larger groups of enemies. Don't forget to collect health power-ups that occasionally spawn to restore your health!
